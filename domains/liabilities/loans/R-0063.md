# Summary

A focused loan management module providing a register, payment recording, and payout functionality for business loans within Ledgius. Operates on the existing GL infrastructure — loans are liability-account balances with structured repayment metadata. Each payment is split into principal (reduces liability) and interest (expense), with GL entries posted automatically.

# Problem / Business Context

Australian SMBs commonly carry multiple financing arrangements: bank loans, equipment finance (chattel mortgages), vehicle finance, and business lines of credit. Without structured loan tracking:
- Interest expense is often miscategorised or forgotten
- Loan balances drift from actual lender statements
- Principal vs interest split is done manually in spreadsheets
- BAS interest deduction claims (W1) lack supporting entries
- Balance sheet shows incorrect liability positions

The real MYOB AO test data (M Bush & Y Laophanna) shows 4 chattel mortgages (Jeep, Toro, Hino, Kobelco) and 2 loans — a common pattern for trades businesses.

# Scope

- Loan register: list of all active and closed loans with current balances
- Payment recording: split each payment into principal + interest with GL posting
- Loan payout: close a loan with final payment + optional early termination fee
- Balance tracking: current balance = original amount − sum of principal payments
- Interest expense tracking: accumulated interest per loan per period

# Out of Scope

- Loan origination / application workflow
- Amortisation schedule generation (future enhancement)
- Variable rate adjustments mid-term
- Loan refinancing
- Credit facility / line of credit draw-down tracking

# Actors / Users

- Business Owner — takes out and pays off loans
- Bookkeeper — records payments, reconciles balances
- Accountant — reviews interest expense, verifies balance sheet positions

# Preconditions

- Chart of accounts includes liability accounts for loans (2-3xxx range)
- Interest expense account exists (e.g. 383/02 Interest paid)
- Bank account configured for payment source

# Functional Requirements

- **LN-001**: System shall maintain a loan register with: name, lender (contact), original amount, current balance, interest rate (annual %), repayment frequency (weekly/fortnightly/monthly/quarterly), repayment amount, start date, maturity date, GL liability account, GL interest expense account, status (active/closed).
- **LN-002**: Recording a loan payment shall split the total into principal and interest components.
- **LN-003**: Principal component shall create GL entries: debit liability account (reduce loan), credit bank account.
- **LN-004**: Interest component shall create GL entries: debit interest expense account, credit bank account.
- **LN-005**: Loan balance shall be computed as: original amount − sum of all principal payments to date.
- **LN-006**: Payment list shall show: date, loan name, principal amount, interest amount, total payment, balance after payment.
- **LN-007**: Loan payout shall: record the final payment (principal = remaining balance), optionally record an early termination fee (debit fee expense, credit bank), set loan status to "closed".
- **LN-008**: Payout form shall show: current balance, payout amount, optional termination fee, total cost, and estimated savings vs completing the original term.
- **LN-009**: Closed loans shall remain in the register for audit trail but be excluded from active balance totals.
- **LN-010**: Register shall show: total active loans, total current balance, total monthly repayment obligation.

# Data / Entities / Fields

Leverages existing tables plus a new metadata table:

| Table | Role |
|-------|------|
| account | Liability GL accounts (category L, long-term liability sub-categories) |
| acc_trans | Individual payment entries (principal + interest legs) |
| transactions | Parent transaction for each payment |
| entity_credit_account | Lender contact (vendor, entity_class=1) |
| **loan_register** (new) | Metadata: name, lender_eca_id, original_amount, interest_rate, repayment_frequency, repayment_amount, start_date, maturity_date, liability_account_id, interest_expense_account_id, status |

# UX / UI Behaviour

- **LN-011**: Loan Register page displays all loans sortable by name, balance, next due date.
- **LN-012**: Record Payment form captures: loan (selector), payment date, total amount, principal/interest split (auto-calculated from rate, overridable).
- **LN-013**: Loan Payout form shows current balance and calculates final payment including optional termination fee.
- **LN-014**: Each page carries an InfoPanel explaining the workflow.

# Validation Rules

- Original loan amount must be positive
- Interest rate must be ≥ 0% (0% for interest-free loans)
- Payment amount cannot exceed current balance + interest
- Payout date must be ≥ loan start date
- Early termination fee cannot be negative

# Security / Permissions

- Minimum role: Bookkeeper (record payments)
- Loan creation and payout requires Bookkeeper or higher
- Register is read-only for Viewer role

# Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] Loan register lists all loans with current balances
- [ ] Record Payment splits principal/interest and creates GL entries
- [ ] Loan balance reduces correctly after principal payments
- [ ] Loan Payout closes the loan and zeros the balance
- [ ] Interest expense accumulates correctly per period
- [ ] Closed loans retained in register for audit
